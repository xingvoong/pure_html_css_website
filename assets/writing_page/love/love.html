Poem: Love
December 23 2019, Xing Voong.

I think I’m in love
I did not expect to,
My brain tries to protect
My heart feels just right.

Maybe I feel love
My brain stops thinking,
My heart skips few beats
This doesn’t seem right.

My brain asks my heart:
“Can you let him in?
You were hurt before
But it’ll be all right”.

Actually I’m in love
My head is so clear
My heart is warming up
He feels that way too.
