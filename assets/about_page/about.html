Hi, my name is Xing, a writer and an aspiring software developer. I was born in Hong Kong at a refugee camp, raised in coffee and black pepper farm town in Southern Vietnam. Currently I'm living in San Francisco where I helped initiating Free City College Program.

I am a senior at UC Berkeley where I study Data Science with a domain in Computer Science. At the school, I discovered my love for technology. I also have met many amazing people who have inspired me to become a better version of myself.

When not talking with a computer while eating trunk food with chopsticks, I enjoy reading, writing and traveling. I write about education, culture and technology. Please check out Writing, Projects and Other page for more.

Currently, I am looking to internships or full time opportunities for after graduation in Dec 2019.

In the meantime, I am always down to meet and collaborate with interesting and fun people.
